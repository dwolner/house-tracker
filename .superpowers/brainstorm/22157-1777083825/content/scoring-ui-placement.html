Where does the scoring personalization UI live?

Where users adjust their factor weights and enter their email for notifications.

A

Side drawer / panel

A "Preferences" button in the header opens a slide-in panel from the right. Sliders inside. Listings re-score live as you drag. Close it to go back to the full view.

Modal / settings page

A "⚙ Preferences" button opens a full-screen modal or dedicated route. More space for sliders, explanations, and the email field. Close to return to listings.

My recommendation: A (side drawer) — seeing listings re-score as you drag is the killer feature here. A drawer preserves the full listing view while making the adjustment feel immediate and tactile.
