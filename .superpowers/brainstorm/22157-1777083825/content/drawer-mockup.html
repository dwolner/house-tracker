Both show locale tabs at the top so you can switch between your PA and SD weights. A uses a continuous slider (precise, but "1.5×" is slightly abstract). B uses named steps (Off / Low / Default / High / Max — maps to 0 / 0.5 / 1 / 1.5 / 2×). B is less precise but immediately legible. My lean: B — "School District: High" reads better than "1.5×" for most people.
